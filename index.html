A Guide to Getting Started

Welcome!


Nook Inc. invites you to create your personal island paradise
on a deserted island brimming with possibility.

First, you'll need to set up your island paradise!


Choose your map







Native Fruit


Your island's native fruit depends on which map
you pick. Visit other islands to collect all the fruits.





Decorate your Island


Create your dream island paradise by
placing town buildings & resident homes
where you want them.





Earn Bells!


Bells are the island currency.
Earn and spend your bells at Nooks Cranny
or use them to expand your island.





Get to know your Residents


Earn friendship points with your residents
as you get to know them and their personalities.





Develop your Island


As you expand and customize your island,
you can earn stars. A five-star island
will have lilly of the valley.





Get Inspired!


Visit your friends' islands through the Dodo Airport
OR explore dream addresses in your sleep.





And that's it!


You're ready to create your dream island paradise!
Your dream getaway begins now,
only on the Nintendo Switch.
